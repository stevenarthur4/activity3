About Me

I am determined in providing high school students with a relevant and burden-lifting experience through the use of scripture. This is no easy feat, particularly since many of today's youth cannot see how ancient text has any relevancy in their modern lives. Hence, one needs a lot of patience, love, boldness, and passion to succeed in seminary teaching.

In my sixth year of teaching, I have now taught seminary full-time at West Seminary and currently at Brighton Seminary. I also sit as a member of the Salt Lake East Area training council, assisting in the training of over 80+ religious educators.

My highest priorities belong to my family: my loving wife, Amy and my six children. I enjoy travelling, reading, and sports. Having lived in Taiwan for two years, I am comfortable in the Mandarin Chinese language.
